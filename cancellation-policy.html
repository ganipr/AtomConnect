Cancellation Policy

Transparent and fair cancellation terms for your peace of mind

Registration Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Atom Conferences, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while covering our operational costs and commitments to venues and vendors.

Full Refund (100%)

  • Cancellations made 30 days or more before the conference start date
  • Conference cancellation by Atom Conferences due to unforeseen circumstances
  • Medical emergencies with proper documentation

Processing: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and will appear on your original payment method within 2-3 billing cycles.

Partial Refund (50%)

  • Cancellations made 15-29 days before the conference start date
  • Conference date changes that conflict with your schedule

Processing: Partial refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

Limited Refund (25%)

  • Cancellations made 7-14 days before the conference start date

Note: This policy helps us manage venue commitments and catering arrangements that are finalized two weeks before events.

No Refund

  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before the conference start date
  • No-shows without prior notification
  • Partial attendance (attending only some days of a multi-day conference)

Alternative: While no refund is available, you may transfer your registration to a colleague (see transfer policy below).

Registration Transfer Policy

We offer flexible transfer options to help accommodate your changing needs:

Attendee Transfer

  • Transfer your registration to another person from your organization
  • Allowed up to 2 days before the conference start date
  • $25 administrative fee applies
  • New attendee must meet any conference prerequisites

Conference Transfer

  • Transfer to another Atom Conference within 12 months
  • Available for cancellations made at least 14 days in advance
  • Price difference may apply if transferring to a higher-priced event
  • $50 administrative fee applies

Special Circumstances

Force Majeure Events

If a conference is cancelled due to circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, pandemics, government restrictions, venue unavailability), we will:

  • Offer a full refund, OR
  • Provide a credit for a future conference (no expiration), OR
  • Convert to virtual attendance if possible

Medical Emergencies

We understand that health emergencies can occur. With proper medical documentation, we may provide:

  • Full refund regardless of timing
  • Complimentary transfer to a future conference
  • Virtual access to recorded sessions when available

Corporate Bookings

Groups of 10 or more attendees may be eligible for modified cancellation terms. Contact our corporate sales team for details.

How to Cancel or Transfer

1

Submit Request

Email us at register@atomconferences.com with your registration details and reason for cancellation or transfer.

2

Confirmation

We'll confirm your request and applicable refund amount within 24 hours during business days.

3

Processing

Refunds are processed according to the timelines specified above. You'll receive email confirmation when complete.

Required Information for Cancellation:

  • Full name and email address used for registration
  • Conference name and date
  • Registration confirmation number
  • Reason for cancellation
  • Preferred refund method (if different from original payment)

Important Notes

Processing Fees

Credit card processing fees (typically 3-4%) are non-refundable on all transactions.

Group Bookings

Cancellations within group bookings are subject to the same individual policies unless specifically negotiated otherwise.

Travel and Accommodation

Atom Conferences is not responsible for any travel, accommodation, or other costs incurred in connection with conference attendance.

Conference Materials

Even if you cannot attend, registered participants may still receive digital conference materials when available.
