Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our conferences

How do I register for a conference?

+

Registration is simple! Browse our upcoming conferences, select the event you're interested in, and click "Register Now." You'll be guided through a secure registration process where you can select your ticket type, provide your information, and complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after registration.

Can I register for multiple conferences at once?

+

Yes! You can register for multiple conferences in a single transaction. During the registration process, you'll have the option to add additional conferences to your cart. We also offer bundle discounts when you register for multiple events.

What information do I need to provide during registration?

+

You'll need to provide your full name, email address, phone number, company/organization name, job title, and billing information. We may also ask for dietary restrictions and accessibility requirements to ensure we can accommodate your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for corporate bookings. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Are there group discounts available?

+

Yes! We offer significant discounts for group registrations. Groups of 3-5 people receive a 10% discount, 6-10 people receive 15%, and groups of 11+ receive 20%. Contact us at register@atomconferences.com to arrange group bookings.

Do you offer early bird pricing?

+

Absolutely! Early bird pricing is available for most conferences and typically offers savings of $100-200. Early bird pricing usually expires 60 days before the conference date. Keep an eye on our website and newsletter for early bird announcements.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

+

Yes, we offer refunds according to our cancellation policy. Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event, 50% refunds up to 14 days before, and 25% refunds up to 7 days before. Please review our complete Cancellation Policy for full details.

What should I expect at a conference?

+

Our conferences feature keynote presentations from industry leaders, interactive workshops, panel discussions, networking sessions, and exhibition areas. You'll receive a detailed agenda upon registration. Most conferences include breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks, plus welcome and closing receptions.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

+

Yes! All registered attendees receive a certificate of attendance that includes the conference name, dates, and number of continuing education credits (where applicable). Certificates are available for download from your attendee portal after the event.

What is the dress code for conferences?

+

Business professional or business casual attire is recommended for most conferences. Specific dress code information will be included in your pre-conference materials. Comfortable shoes are recommended as there may be significant walking between session rooms.

Are meals and refreshments included?

+

Yes! Conference registration includes breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks on all conference days. We accommodate dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other special dietary needs. Please specify your requirements during registration.

Will WiFi be available at the venue?

+

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout all conference venues. Network credentials will be provided in your welcome packet or displayed at registration areas.

Are presentations recorded or available afterward?

+

Selected presentations may be recorded with speaker permission. When available, recordings and presentation materials are shared with registered attendees via email within 48 hours after the conference. Not all sessions are recorded due to confidentiality and speaker preferences.

Is there a mobile app for the conference?

+

For larger conferences, we provide a mobile app that includes the agenda, speaker bios, venue maps, networking features, and real-time updates. Download instructions are sent to registered attendees one week before the event.

Can I transfer my registration to someone else?

+

Yes! Registration transfers are allowed up to 7 days before the conference date. To transfer your registration, please contact us at register@atomconferences.com with the new attendee's information. A $25 administrative fee applies to all transfers.

Do you provide accommodation recommendations?

+

Yes! We provide a list of recommended hotels near each conference venue, often with special discounted rates for our attendees. Accommodation information is included in your confirmation email and pre-conference materials.

How do I become a speaker at your conferences?

+

We welcome speaker applications! Please send your proposal including your bio, topic summary, and presentation outline to speakers@atomconferences.com. We typically open speaker applications 6-8 months before each conference and notify selected speakers within 30 days of application.
